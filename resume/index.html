I'm Yusuke Urameshi, former street punk turned Spirit Detective. I specialize in getting thrown into impossible situations and punching my way through them with results. With experience handling paranormal threats, rogue apparitions, and interdimensional politics, I bring high energy (literally and figuratively), solid instincts, and zero patience for nonsense. I’m all about results—whether that’s solving a case, protecting people, or training under a cranky martial arts master in the middle of nowhere.
